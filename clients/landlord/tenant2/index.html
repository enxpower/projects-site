签约前两项最终确认

Subject: Final Clarifications Before Signing

主题：签约前最终确认

Dear Landlord

Thank you again for your cooperation and revisions. We appreciate your flexibility and would like to finalize the agreement soon.

再次感谢您的配合与修改，我们非常感谢您的灵活调整，也希望尽快完成签约。

Before signing, we would like to clarify the following points to ensure long-term clarity and mutual comfort.

在签署前，我们希望就以下事项做最终确认，以确保长期清晰稳定、双方安心。

  1. Utilities and Internet Cost Sharing 1）水电气及网络费用分摊

For the 70% utilities allocation, we kindly request confirmation that:

关于 70% 水电气费用分摊，我们希望确认：

  • Copies of the original utility bills will be provided;
  • 70% reflects a mutually agreed proportion;
  • Payment will be due within 7 days after receiving the bill copy.
  • 会提供原始账单副本；
  • 70% 为双方确认的分摊比例；
  • 收到账单副本后 7 天内支付。

Regarding Internet service, we kindly request confirmation of the current plan and monthly cost. Any upgrade or change that increases the cost should be mutually agreed upon before implementation.

关于网络服务，我们希望确认当前套餐及月费用。如涉及升级或价格上涨，应在实施前经双方协商同意。

  1. Landlord Liability Clause 2）房东免责条款

For the clause stating the landlord is not responsible for loss or damage “notwithstanding the cause,” we respectfully suggest clarifying that this does not apply where loss is caused by landlord negligence or breach of the Residential Tenancy Act.

关于“无论何种原因房东不承担责任”的条款，我们建议明确：若损失由房东的疏忽或违反《Residential Tenancy Act》导致，则不适用该免责条款。

This clarification would align the agreement with standard tenancy practice.

此澄清将使协议更符合标准租赁实践。

  1. Exterior Maintenance (Lawn and Snow Removal) 3）户外维护（草坪及除雪）

As the property includes both the main unit and the secondary suite occupied by the landlord, we respectfully request that all exterior maintenance responsibilities, including lawn care, garden maintenance, and snow removal, be managed by the landlord.

鉴于该物业包含主体及由房东居住的偏房，我们希望所有户外维护责任（包括草坪养护、绿化维护及除雪）由房东统一负责。

We propose revising the clause to state:

我们建议将条款修改为：

“The Landlord shall be solely responsible for all exterior maintenance of the property, including lawn care, garden maintenance, and snow removal, in accordance with applicable municipal bylaws and safety standards.”

“房东应全权负责物业的所有户外维护，包括草坪养护、绿化维护及除雪，并符合相关市政法规及安全标准。”

  1. Parking Confirmation 4）停车确认

We would like to confirm that parking for one standard passenger van is permitted on the property in compliance with applicable municipal and community regulations.

我们希望确认可在物业内停放一辆标准乘用型 VAN，并符合市政及社区相关规定。

We believe these clarifications will ensure a clear and cooperative tenancy relationship.

我们相信以上确认将有助于建立清晰、合作的租赁关系。

Thank you very much for your understanding.

感谢您的理解。

Sincerely,

ANDY
