Addendum to Residential Tenancy Agreement

住宅租赁协议补充条款（修订整合版）

For the property located at:

物业地址：___________

Landlord and Tenant agree that notices about the Tenancy may be served electronically via agreed email addresses.

房东与租客同意，租赁相关通知可通过双方确认的电子邮箱发送。

The terms and conditions in this Addendum are included in and form part of the Tenancy Agreement.

本补充条款构成租赁协议不可分割的一部分。

1. Moving In Inspection

1. 入住验房

The landlord and the tenant agree that at move-in, both parties may exchange photos via email for record purposes. These records may be used for comparison at move-out.

房东与租客同意，入住时可通过电子邮件交换照片留存记录，作为退租时对比依据。

2. Early Termination of Fixed Term

2. 固定租期提前解约

If the Tenant requests early termination of a fixed-term tenancy, compensation shall reflect actual vacancy loss only. The Landlord shall make reasonable efforts to re-rent the premises promptly and provide supporting documentation of any actual loss.

若租客要求提前终止固定租期，赔偿仅限于实际空置损失。房东应合理尽快重新出租，并提供实际损失的相关证明。

3. No Smoking

3. 禁烟

Absolutely no smoking (including tobacco, vaporizer, marijuana/cannabis products) is permitted anywhere on the premises.

本物业任何区域严禁吸烟（包括烟草、电子烟、大麻等）。

4. Marijuana Cultivation

4. 大麻种植

Tenant agrees not to grow marijuana plants. Any enforcement action shall be in accordance with the Residential Tenancy Act.

租客不得种植大麻。如有违反，应依据《Residential Tenancy Act》依法处理。

5. Landlord Liability

5. 房东责任

The Landlord shall not be responsible for loss or damage to the Tenant’s property, except where such loss or damage is caused by the Landlord’s negligence or breach of the Residential Tenancy Act.

房东对租客财产损失不承担责任，但若损失由房东的疏忽或违反《Residential Tenancy Act》导致，则不适用本免责条款。

6. Use of Property

6. 房屋用途

The property shall be used for private residential purposes only. Remote home office work is permitted, provided it does not cause disturbance or violate applicable laws.

本物业仅用于私人居住用途。允许安静的居家远程办公，但不得扰民或违反相关法律。

Short-term rentals (including Airbnb) are prohibited without written consent of the Landlord. Immediate family members residing as household occupants are permitted.

未经书面同意不得进行短租（包括 Airbnb）。直系家庭成员作为家庭成员居住是允许的。

7. Pets

7. 宠物

No pets allowed unless otherwise agreed in writing.

未经书面同意不得饲养宠物。

8. Cleanliness & Hazard

8. 清洁与危险物品

Tenant shall maintain reasonable cleanliness and sanitary standards within the rental unit and properly dispose of garbage.

租客应保持合理清洁及卫生标准，并妥善处理垃圾。

Tenant shall not store hazardous or dangerous items.

不得存放危险物品。

Tenant shall immediately notify the Landlord of fire, water escape, gas leak, or other hazards.

发生火灾、漏水、燃气泄漏等情况应立即通知房东。

Tenant is responsible for damages caused by negligence or intentional acts of Tenant or Tenant’s guests.

租客或其访客因疏忽或故意造成的损坏由租客负责。

9. Exterior Maintenance

9. 户外维护

The Landlord shall be solely responsible for all exterior maintenance of the property, including lawn care, garden maintenance, and snow removal, in accordance with applicable municipal bylaws and safety standards.

房东应全权负责物业的所有户外维护，包括草坪养护、绿化维护及除雪，并符合市政法规及安全标准。

10. Vacancy Notification

10. 长期离开通知

If the Tenant leaves the property vacant for more than one week, the Tenant shall notify the Landlord.

若租客连续离开超过一周，应通知房东。

Interior temperature shall be maintained at a reasonable level to prevent damage.

室内温度应保持合理水平以防止损坏。

11. Keys and Access Devices

11. 钥匙与门禁

Lost or damaged keys, FOBs, or garage openers shall be replaced at Tenant’s cost.

钥匙、门禁卡或车库遥控器遗失或损坏由租客承担更换费用。

Unauthorized duplication of keys is prohibited.

未经书面许可不得复制钥匙。

12. Security Deposit Settlement

12. 押金结算

The rental unit must be returned in reasonably clean condition, comparable to move-in condition, subject to normal wear and tear.

退租时房屋应达到合理清洁状态（扣除正常损耗）。

Any cleaning deduction must reflect actual and reasonable costs supported by receipts.

如扣除清洁费用，必须为实际发生且合理费用，并提供凭证。

Security deposit shall be returned in accordance with the Residential Tenancy Act.

押金退还应依据《Residential Tenancy Act》执行。

13. End of Tenancy

13. 退租

Tenant shall vacate the rental unit by 1:00 PM on the day the tenancy ends unless otherwise agreed in writing.

租客应在退租当日下午1:00前交付房屋，除非双方书面另有约定。

Property showings shall comply with the Residential Tenancy Act requirements.

带看房屋须符合《Residential Tenancy Act》相关规定。

A forwarding address must be provided in writing for return of security deposit.

租客须书面提供转寄地址以便退还押金。

14. Utilities & Internet

14. 水电气及网络

Tenant shall pay 70% of electricity, natural gas, water, and internet costs, reflecting a mutually agreed proportion.

租客承担电费、燃气费、水费及网络费用的70%，为双方约定分摊比例。

Copies of original utility bills shall be provided. Payment shall be due within seven (7) days after receipt of bill copies.

房东应提供原始账单副本，租客应在收到副本后7天内支付。

Internet service plan type and current monthly cost shall be confirmed. Any upgrade or change increasing cost must be mutually agreed upon before implementation.

网络套餐类型及当前月费用应明确确认。任何导致费用上涨的升级或变更，须双方事先协商同意。

Tenant shall maintain tenant insurance throughout the tenancy.

租客须在租期内维持租客保险。

15. Parking

15. 停车

Parking for one standard passenger van is permitted on the property in compliance with applicable municipal and community regulations.

允许在物业内停放一辆标准乘用型 VAN，并符合市政及社区相关规定。

16. Electric Vehicle Charging Facility

16. 电动车充电设施

The Landlord agrees that a functional electric vehicle charging facility (compatible with standard Level 2 residential charging) shall be installed and fully operational prior to the Tenant’s move-in date.

房东同意，在租客入住前，应安装并确保可正常使用的电动车充电设施（兼容标准 Level 2 住宅充电）。

The charging installation shall comply with applicable electrical codes and safety standards.

该充电设施的安装应符合相关电气规范及安全标准。
