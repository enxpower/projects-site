Clarifications and Proposed Revisions to Tenancy Agreement

Dear Landlord,

Thank you again for preparing the tenancy agreement. We sincerely appreciate the opportunity to rent the main unit and look forward to establishing a stable and respectful tenancy relationship.

再次感谢您准备租赁协议。我们非常珍惜租住主体部分的机会，也期待建立长期、稳定、相互尊重的租赁关系。

Before signing, we would like to clarify and propose the following revisions to ensure clarity, fairness, and long-term stability for both parties.

在签署前，我们希望就以下条款进行澄清和修改，以确保双方权责明确、公平合理、长期稳定。

1. Fixed Term Expiry Clause

1. 固定租期到期条款

We kindly request selecting Option D under Section 2 of the RTB-1 (fixed term automatically converts to month-to-month).

我们希望在 RTB-1 第 2 部分选择 D（固定租期到期后自动转为月租）。

If Option E (mandatory move-out at end of term) is preferred, please clearly state the legally permitted reason and corresponding Residential Tenancy Regulation section number, and ensure both parties initial this section as required.

若选择 E（到期必须搬离），请明确写明法规允许的理由及对应条款编号，并按规定由双方 initials 确认。

2. Early Termination (Addendum #2)

2. 提前解约条款（附加条款第2条）

We respectfully request removing the fixed one-month compensation clause.

我们建议删除“固定赔偿一个月租金”的条款。

Instead, early termination compensation should reflect actual vacancy loss only, meaning compensation applies only for the period the unit remains unrented. The landlord will make reasonable efforts to re-rent promptly and provide supporting documentation.

改为按实际空置损失结算，即仅在房屋未能及时重新出租期间承担合理损失，并由房东合理尽快重新出租并提供相关证明。

3. Business Use Restriction (Addendum #6)

3. 商业用途限制（附加条款第6条）

We agree the property should not be used for retail, customer visits, commercial storage, or any unlawful or nuisance activity.

我们同意房屋不得用于对外经营、接待客户、仓储或任何违法、扰民用途。

However, we request clarification that quiet remote work from home is permitted, provided it does not disturb the property or violate zoning laws.

但希望明确：安静的居家远程办公属于允许范围，前提是不影响邻里且不违反相关规定。

4. Professional Cleaning Fee (Addendum #12)

4. 专业清洁费用（附加条款第12条）

We respectfully request removal of the fixed minimum $800 cleaning charge.

我们建议删除“最低 $800 清洁费”的固定条款。

Any cleaning deduction should reflect actual and reasonable cleaning costs, supported by receipts or invoices.

改为按实际发生且合理的清洁费用扣除，并提供发票或凭证。

5. Utilities – 70% Cost Sharing (Addendum #18)

5. 水电气费用 70% 分摊（附加条款第18条）

We kindly request clarification of the basis for the 70% allocation (e.g., square footage or mutually agreed proportion).

希望明确 70% 分摊依据（如面积比例或双方约定比例）。

Copies of original utility bills should be provided.

请提供原始账单副本。

Payment should be due within 7 days after receiving bill copies.

付款期限建议为收到副本后 7 天内。

For Internet service, please clarify the plan type, cost cap, upgrade policy, and how future price increases will be handled.

关于网络服务，请明确套餐类型、费用上限、升级规则及未来涨价的处理方式。

Additionally, we would like to ask whether installation of separate electricity and gas meters could be considered in the future to ensure long-term transparency.

此外，希望未来可以考虑加装独立电表和气表，以确保长期透明。

6. Entry and Showing (Addendum #17)

6. 入屋与看房（附加条款第17条）

Any entry or showing of the property must comply with the Residential Tenancy Act and RTB-1 requirements, including proper written notice and permitted time windows.

所有入屋或带人看房行为必须遵守《Residential Tenancy Act》及 RTB-1 关于书面通知及时间限制的规定。

7. Move-Out Time

7. 退租时间

We kindly request adjusting the move-out time from 11:00am to 1:00pm, consistent with RTB standard terms, or alternatively that the move-out time be based on a mutually agreed written move-out inspection schedule.

建议将退租时间由上午 11:00 改为 下午 1:00（符合 RTB 标准条款），或改为以双方书面确认的验房时间为准。

8. Immediate Family Members

8. 直系家庭成员居住问题

Immediate family members (parents, siblings, close relatives) residing with us should be considered household occupants rather than temporary visitors, provided occupancy remains reasonable and compliant with safety standards.

直系家庭成员（父母、兄弟姐妹或近亲）如与我们同住，应视为家庭成员而非访客，前提是居住人数合理并符合安全规范。

We believe these revisions will help ensure a clear, fair, and cooperative tenancy relationship.

我们相信以上修改有助于建立清晰、公平、合作的租赁关系。

Thank you very much for your time and consideration.

感谢您的时间与理解。

Sincerely,

ANDY
