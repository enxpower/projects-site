Attachment A – RFP Compliance Matrix

Project: OAK VOLT STORAGE (9.99 MW)

Document No.: CA-OAK01-RFP-A1

Revision: Rev 1.0

Date: October 15, 2025

Prepared by: Energize Solutions Inc. (System Integrator)

Section A – Technical Requirements

A1 – System Capacity: 10 MW AC

Energize Solutions confirms compliance with a nominal 10 MW AC output rating, implemented through four parallel PCS units rated at 2.5 MW each. This configuration provides redundancy and maintains full power delivery even under one-unit outage (N-1).

Compliance: ✅ Compliant

Reference: RFP §2 Project Scope [Updated 251013 Response of RFP and Missed Info about RFP.docx L21-L24]

A2 – Configuration: 3 cabins / 6 battery containers

The proposed system architecture will use three integrated battery enclosures, each containing two modular battery containers. The system design aligns with the client’s 3-cabin / 6-container structure for logistics and serviceability.

Compliance: ✅ Compliant

Reference: RFP §2 Project Scope [L25-L28]

A3 – DC Bus Voltage: 1000 V / 1500 V (to be confirmed)

Energize Solutions proposes a 1500 VDC nominal bus configuration to achieve improved round-trip efficiency, reduced cable losses, and full compliance with UL 9540 / UL 1973 design limits.

Compliance: ✅ Compliant

Reference: Missed Info §1 Technical Questions [L97-L100]

A4 – AC Output Voltage: 12.47 kV

The PCS–transformer assembly steps up from 630 V to 12.47 kV at the collector bus, matching the interconnection voltage.

Compliance: ✅ Compliant

Reference: CSL01 Collector SLD [L94-L95]

A5 – Point of Interconnection (POI): 69 kV

Energize Solutions acknowledges that the interconnection is at 69 kV; the 12.47 kV collector output will interface via the substation transformer.

Compliance: ✅ Compliant

Reference: PSL01 Substation SLD [L457-L471]

A6 – System Architecture: Standalone BESS – AC Coupled

Four PCS + four step-up transformers feeding a 12.47 kV ring bus, forming an AC-coupled topology consistent with grid standards.

Compliance: ✅ Compliant

Reference: Missed Info §1 [L105-L106]

A7 – Operating Temperature Range – Devine, TX

Design validated for ambient up to 50°C with HVAC redundancy and derating.

Compliance: ✅ Compliant

Reference: Missed Info §1 [L115-L116]

A8 – Fire Safety – NFPA 68/69, UL 1973

Enclosures integrate smoke detection, aerosol suppression, and controlled venting per NFPA 68/69; all modules UL 1973 certified.

Compliance: ✅ Compliant

Reference: Missed Info §1 [L117-L118]

A9 – EMS: Not needed

Energize Solutions acknowledges EMS exclusion; BMS/PCS controls ensure autonomous safety and performance, with optional SCADA interface.

Compliance: ✅ Compliant

Reference: Missed Info §1 [L123-L124]

A10 – Black Start / Islanding: Not required

No black-start or islanding features required; system remains grid-following.

Compliance: ✅ Compliant

Reference: Missed Info §1 [L119-L120]
